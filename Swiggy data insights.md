# Swiggy-Data-Insights
Built a  dynamic Excel dashboard to analyze Swiggy data, leveraging KPIs, pivot tables, slicers, and lookup functions to transform raw data into meaningful business insights, highlighting revenue trends, customer behavior, price vs rating analysis, weekend patterns, and state-level performance.


Data Insights – Interactive Excel Dashboard

This project presents a fully interactive Excel dashboard built using a Swiggy dataset to analyze food delivery trends, customer preferences, and business performance. It integrates data cleaning, transformation, and advanced analytics to convert raw data into actionable business insights through an intuitive and user-friendly interface.

Key Features
 
📌 KPI Metrics
Total Revenue
Average Order Value (AOV)
Average Rating
Maximum Order Price
Total Ratings Count

📊 Advanced Data Analysis

State-wise & Location-wise Sales Performance
Top 10 States by Total Revenue & Ratings
Top Locations by Total Sales (Price Aggregation)
Monthly & Daily Sales Trend Analysis
Top 50 High-Revenue Dates Identification
Weekend vs Weekday Sales Comparison
Most Ordered & Most Popular Dishes Analysis
Top 10 Dishes based on Revenue & Ratings
Famous Dishes based on Customer Preferences
Price vs Rating Comparative Analysis
Highest Sales Trend Identification using Date Analysis
Rating vs Rating Count Distribution Analysis

📈 Visualizations

Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts for dynamic aggregation
Bar, Line, and Pie Charts for trend comparison
Geo Map Visualization (State-wise Revenue Distribution)
Location-based Price Analysis using Map Charts
Comparative Charts (Price vs Ratings, State vs Sales, etc.)
Time-Series Analysis (Daily & Monthly Trends)

🎛️ Interactivity

Slicers for dynamic filtering (Day-wise, State-wise, Location-wise)
Interactive dashboard controls for real-time exploration
User-driven filtering for deep dive analysis
Clean, structured, and visually optimized dashboard layout

📌 Project Objective

To analyze Swiggy data and generate insights into:

Customer ordering behavior
Revenue distribution across states and locations
Product (dish) performance and popularity
Time-based sales patterns and trends
Business performance optimization opportunities

💡 Key Insights Generated

Identified top 10 states contributing highest revenue and ratings
Discovered top 50 revenue-generating dates and peak sales periods
Highlighted weekend sales dominance over weekdays
Identified top-performing locations based on total order value
Analyzed most ordered and most popular dishes

Identified highest sales growth trends using date-wise analysis

This dashboard provides a comprehensive analytical view of Swiggy business data, enabling better understanding of sales performance, customer preferences, and regional trends.
